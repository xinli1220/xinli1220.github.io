A Brief Introduction of Xin

Xin is a student at the University of Florida. She is seeking her master degree in Telecommunication at the College of Journalism and Communications. In the past few years, Xin has been a:

  • News correspondent
  • Photographer
  • Writer
  • Social media operator
  • World traveler

Xin is a “cult” of documentary and film production, who loves documentary as it has the power to people’s perceptions of the world. Xin hopes one day she could produce some documentaries about her motherland from the perspective of an investigative journalist. See Xin’s short documentary debut at the Multimedia Gallery.

A traveler, who earned her bachelor degree in the U.S. and acquired her first formal internship in London. While Xin was in the U.S., she traveled to many states for documentary productions with her Documentary Film Society friends. All the travelings have broadened Xin’s views of the world and have made her open-minded. Therefore, Xin would love to travel for work whenever you need.

A “readaholic”, who loves reading and considers herself as an omnivore as she reads books of pure literature, science and critical essays. Favorite readings: The New Yorker, National Geographic, Los Angeles Times Framework, books of Carson McCullers and classic Chinese novels.

A dedicated writer, who can not only write in English, but is good at Chinese writing as well. She has a blog where she combines her love for browsing photography websites and love for writing. For Xin, different stories by different languages deliver different worldviews. A team player, who has valuable experience in efficient teamwork. Xin is a patient and critical listener and a competent communicator, who will be a good team player you could have for your organization.Xin is fascinated by visual media while she is eager to learn more about the world of non-fiction writing. She aspires to have a career in the multimedia industry.
